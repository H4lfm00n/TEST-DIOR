# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Car enthusiasts and Supra fans who already know and care about the car. They come for depth — generation breakdowns, spec details, the 2JZ mythology. They have taste and they'll notice if the design is cheap.

## Product Purpose

A premium editorial showcase for the Toyota Supra. Part reference site, part fan tribute. The experience should make someone who loves this car feel like the site was made *for* them — not by a template. Success means a visitor scrolls to the bottom and feels like the design respected the subject matter.

## Brand Personality

Cinematic. Bold. Refined.

Not loud for loudness's sake — bold with intention. The restraint of a Porsche editorial paired with the presence of a car that defined an era.

## Reference

**Porsche.com** — large photography commanding the viewport, typographic hierarchy that breathes, premium restraint. The car sells itself; the design simply doesn't get in the way.

## Anti-references

- **Generic car forum**: cluttered, busy, zero visual hierarchy
- **Fast & Furious fan aesthetic**: neon, flames, over-the-top tuner energy — the Supra is beyond that
- **Beige / warm AI-default neutrals**: sand, cream, parchment — no warmth-by-default
- **Corporate brochure**: stiff, formal, personality-free

## Design Principles

1. **The car is the hero.** Photography and scale do the heavy lifting. Design clears the way.
2. **Restraint is not timidity.** Premium feel comes from what's removed, not what's added.
3. **Editorial confidence.** Type is big, hierarchy is clear, nothing competes for attention.
4. **Mechanical soul.** The precision of the 2JZ engine — overbuilt, exact, purposeful — is a design reference, not just a subject.
5. **Earn every detail.** No decoration without function. No animation without reason.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA minimum. Full dark/light theme support already in place. `prefers-reduced-motion` respected throughout. High-contrast text on all backgrounds — especially critical on the hero where text sits over imagery.
